Method & caveats

What is read. For each wrapper the page calls getSpokeConfig(assetId, spoke) on the Core Hub for the deposit cap and the active/halted flags, plus totalAssets() and maxDeposit(address) on the ERC-4626 wrapper itself. All three are eth_call at latest over public RPC, in your browser — no backend, no API key.
How to read it. Caps are governance-set per spoke. Available is the room left before the cap, as the wrapper itself reports it. Bands: open under 40% filled, filling 40–90%, at cap above 90%. A wrapper with a cap of 2^40−1 is shown as uncapped; one with a cap of 0 is listed as disabled.
Units. Figures are in native token units, not dollars — stablecoins are ≈ $1, but AVAX, BTC and ETH are not, so the stablecoin-headroom KPI sums only the $-pegged wrappers.
Spoke set. The wrapper list is embedded from a hub scan on 2026-07-15 — a spoke listed after that date will not appear until the list is regenerated with aave_v4_spoke_caps.py. Every number attached to a listed spoke is live. Click any address to copy it.
