Where the data comes from

all read client-side, in your browser
On-chain
Aave v4 Hub contracts read with eth_call over public RPC (publicnode, drpc, api.avax.network) — spoke configs, caps, reserve rates and borrowable liquidity.
Protocol APIs
Fluid vaults api.fluid.instadapp.io for per-token rates, staking APR, rewards and pool reserves; Merkl api.merkl.xyz for live incentive campaigns and their end dates.
What is not live
Anything a page cannot fetch from the browser is labelled with a dated snapshot badge and the date it was read. If a badge has no date, the number is live.
